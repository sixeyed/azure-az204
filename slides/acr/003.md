# Why Use a Private Registry?

<div class="mt-8">

<iconify-icon icon="mdi:check-circle" class="text-green-500" /> **Security and access control** - Keep your proprietary images private and control who can access them

<iconify-icon icon="mdi:check-circle" class="text-green-500" /> **Regional deployment** - Store images in the same Azure region as your compute services for faster pulls and lower latency

<iconify-icon icon="mdi:check-circle" class="text-green-500" /> **Azure integration** - Leverage Azure Active Directory for authentication and role-based access control

<iconify-icon icon="mdi:check-circle" class="text-green-500" /> **Compliance** - Meet organizational requirements for where container images are stored

</div>
