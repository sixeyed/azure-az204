# What Are APIM Policies?

<div class="mt-8">

<iconify-icon icon="mdi:check-circle" class="text-green-500" /> **Input policies** - These fire before the backend gets called and can alter the request or even avoid calling the backend at all
<iconify-icon icon="mdi:check-circle" class="text-green-500" /> **Output policies** - These fire after the backend has been called and can alter the response before it gets sent to the client

</div>
